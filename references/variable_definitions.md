## Variable Definitions

### Explanatory Variable (Group Variable)
• **Explanatory Variable: NoMilkDrinking**  
• It represents the **frequency of milk drinking during the past 7 days.**  
• The original variable was measured on a 7-point ordinal scale (1–7).  
• For analysis purposes, it was recoded into three levels of milk drinking frequency: **0 = no milk drinking, 1 = occasional milk  
  drinking, and 2 = frequent milk drinking.**

### Response Variable
• **Response Variable: HowTallAreYouWithoutShoesInMeters**  
• It represents **height measured in meters without shoes.** 
• This variable is continuous and was used in its original form without any transformation.
