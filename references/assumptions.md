Independence of Observations
In this project, each participant represents a separate student in the dataset, and each student's height measurement is independent of others.
Normality
The distribution of height within each milk consumption group (no milk drinking, occasional  milk drinking, frequent  milk drinking) is assumed to be approximately normal.
Homogeneity of Variance
The variability of height is assumed to be similar across the three milk consumption groups.
Continuous Dependent Variable
Height (measured in meters) is a continuous variable measured on a ratio scale.
