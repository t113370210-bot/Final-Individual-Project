# Assumptions of One-way ANOVA

## 1. Independence of Observations
In this project, each participant represents a separate student in the dataset, and each student's height measurement is independent of others.

## 2. Normality
The distribution of height within each level of milk drinking frequency (no milk drinking, occasional milk drinking, frequent milk drinking) is assumed to be approximately normal.

## 3. Homogeneity of Variance
The variability of height is assumed to be similar across the three levels of milk drinking frequency.

## 4. Continuous Dependent Variable
Height (measured in meters) is a continuous variable measured on a ratio scale.
