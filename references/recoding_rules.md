# Recoding Rules

## Explanatory Variable (Group Variable)
• Explanatory Variable: **NoMilkDrinking**  
• What it measures: Frequency of milk drinking during the past 7 days (7-point ordinal scale, 1–7, where higher values indicate higher drinking frequency).

• **Recoded Rule:  
  0 = no milk drinking (original code 1), did not drink milk during the past 7 days.  
  1 = occasional milk drinking (original codes 2–3), at most six glasses during the past 7 days.  
  2 = frequent milk drinking (original codes 4–7), at least one glass per day.**  

## Response Variable
• Response Variable: **HowTallAreYouWithoutShoesInMeters**  
• What it measures: Height measured in meters without shoes.                                                                             
• **Recoded Rule: Not applicable, continuous variable retained as original.**
